Our Story

Two Decades of Growing
Something Worth Sharing

Monte Carlo Farms started as a dream and a few rows of Hass avocado trees on the slopes above Santa Paula. We're a two-person operation — still doing most of the work ourselves, still hands-on in the grove every single week.

We believe that food grown with care tastes different. No shortcuts, no middlemen — just avocados picked at peak ripeness and delivered straight to the people who love them.

When you order from Monte Carlo Farms, you're not buying from a distributor. You're buying directly from us. That matters to us — and we think it'll matter to you too.

Our Home

Rooted in the Heart
of Santa Paula

Santa Paula is known as the avocado capital of the world — and we're proud to call it home. Nestled in the Santa Clara River Valley at the foot of the Topatopa Mountains, our farm benefits from a climate that's been growing avocados for over a century.

This is a small town where neighbors know each other by name and the land has been farmed for generations. The historic downtown, the orange groves, the mountains — they're all part of what makes this place, and what makes our avocados taste the way they do.
